    SAM-BA reset applet

    Resetting the target

    The reset applet writes into the Control Register (CR) of the Reset Controller (RSTC) to reset the SAM MPU device on the target. Depending on the board design, other peripherals such as external NVM may or may not be reset as well.

    sam-ba -p <port> -d <device> -a reset

$ sam-ba -p serial -d sama5d2 -a reset
